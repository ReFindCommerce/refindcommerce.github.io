Wallet Tracker Setup Notes for Everyday Carry

A wallet tracker works best when it is treated as part of the daily carry routine, not just as a last-minute lost-wallet tool.

Before leaving home, check that the tracker is seated flat in the wallet, the phone app can see it, and the wallet has not been left in a coat, gym bag, or travel pouch from the day before.

For travel days, keep the wallet in the same pocket or bag section each time you pass through security, hotel check-in, taxis, and cafes. A simple repeated location habit makes the tracker alert more useful because there are fewer possible places to check.
