- Where Should You Put a Tracker Card in Your Wallet?

- A tracker card works best when it is easy to carry, protected, and not buried somewhere that makes it harder to connect.

- Put a tracker card in a flat card slot where it sits securely and is not bent or tightly squeezed.

- A good first choice is an outer or easy-to-reach card slot inside your wallet, purse, or card holder.

- If you use a metal wallet, place the tracker card away from the metal plates where possible and test it with your phone before relying on it day to day.
