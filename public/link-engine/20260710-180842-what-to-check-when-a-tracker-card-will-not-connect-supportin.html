# What to Check When a Tracker Card Will Not Connect? - Supporting Guide

What to Check When a Tracker Card Will Not Connect If a tracker card will not connect, check that the card is compatible with your phone, your phone?s Bluetooth is on, the correct app or network is being used, the tracker is in pairing mode, the card is close to the phone, and... More

- For an Apple-compatible tracker card, also check that Apple Find My is set up correctly on your iPhone.

- A tracker card is useful only when it is set up properly.

- If yours will not connect, a few simple checks can often solve the problem before you assume the card is faulty.

- Apple users should also check their Apple Find My settings.

- Check the tracker card is compatible with your phone Not every tracker card works with every phone or app.

- If you try to pair a card in the wrong app or with the wrong type of phone, setup may fail even if the card itself is working.

Small tracking accessories are most useful when the setup is simple, compatible with the customer device, and easy to keep with the item being tracked.
