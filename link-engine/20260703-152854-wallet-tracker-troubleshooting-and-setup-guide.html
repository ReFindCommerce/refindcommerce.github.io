<h2>Before you start</h2>

<p>

Most setup problems come down to model compatibility, charge level, phone settings, or trying to pair the tracker before the phone is ready. Before you troubleshoot, confirm the basics below.

</p>

<ul>

<li>

<strong>Confirm the tracker matches your phone ecosystem.</strong>

The <a href="https://easytag.app/products/easytag-apple-wallet-tracker">Wallet Tracker for Apple</a> is a wallet tracking card compatible with Apple Find My devices. The <a href="https://easytag.app/products/easytag-samsung-wallet-tracker">Wallet Tracker for Samsung</a> is compatible with Samsung and Galaxy devices.

</li>

<li>

<strong>Charge the tracker before pairing.</strong>

If your original cable is missing or damaged, use the <a href="https://easytag.app/products/easytag-card-tracker-replacement-charging-cable">Additional / Replacement easyTag Charging Cable</a>, a USB-C to proprietary magnetic charging cable for Apple and Samsung easyTag card trackers.

</li>

<li>

<strong>Update and prepare your phone.</strong>

Make sure your phone is signed in, connected to the internet, and has Bluetooth and location settings enabled for the tracking setup you are using.

</li>

<li>

<strong>Pair one tracker at a time.</strong>

Keep the easyTag card close to your phone during setup and move other unpaired trackers away to avoid confusion.

</li>

<li>

<strong>Use the included model instructions for exact button, reset, or indicator behavior.</strong>

This guide covers general setup and troubleshooting. If your product packaging or product page gives specific instructions, follow those first.

</li>

</ul>

<p class="note">

A tracker is a helpful backup, not a guarantee. No wallet tracker can prevent every loss or theft, so continue to keep wallets, passports, and bags secure.

</p>

<section id="apple-setup">

<h2>Setup notes for Wallet Tracker for Apple</h2>

<p>

The <a href="https://easytag.app/products/easytag-apple-wallet-tracker">Wallet Tracker for Apple</a> is an easyTag wallet tracking card compatible with Apple Find My devices. It is intended for people who want a wallet-friendly Apple tracker option.

</p>

<h3>If your Apple device does not detect the tracker</h3>

<ul>

<li>Charge the easyTag card before trying again.</li>

<li>Keep the tracker close to your Apple device during setup.</li>

<li>Make sure Bluetooth, location settings, and internet connectivity are enabled.</li>

<li>Confirm you are using the Apple-compatible easyTag model, not the Samsung-compatible model.</li>

<li>Restart your phone and reopen the tracking setup flow.</li>

<li>If the tracker was previously set up, check the model instructions for how to remove it from the previous account before pairing again.</li>

</ul>

<h3>If setup starts but does not complete</h3>

<ul>

<li>Keep the tracker and phone close together until setup finishes.</li>

<li>Stay on a stable internet connection.</li>

<li>Do not try to add multiple trackers at the same time.</li>

<li>Follow any prompts shown on your Apple device before closing the setup screen.</li>

</ul>

<section id="charging">

<h2>Charging and cable checks</h2>

<p>

Apple and Samsung easyTag card trackers use a USB-C to proprietary magnetic charging cable. If the original cable is unavailable, the <a href="https://easytag.app/products/easytag-card-tracker-replacement-charging-cable">Additional / Replacement easyTag Charging Cable</a> is made for Apple and Samsung easyTag card trackers.

</p>

<h3>Charging troubleshooting steps</h3>

<ol>

<li>

<strong>Use the correct cable.</strong>

Proprietary magnetic connectors can look similar, so use the easyTag cable intended for the card tracker.

</li>

<li>

<strong>Check the USB-C power source.</strong>

Try a known working USB-C port or power adapter.

</li>

<li>

<strong>Inspect the magnetic connection point.</strong>

Look for dust, lint, or debris. If needed, gently clean with a dry, soft cloth.

</li>

<li>

<strong>Let it charge before setup.</strong>

If the tracker has been unused for a while, give it uninterrupted charging time before pairing.

</li>

<li>

<strong>Do not force the connector.</strong>

Align the magnetic connector according to the product instructions.

</li>

</ol>

<p>

If charging problems continue after trying a known working power source and the correct cable, review the product instructions for model-specific guidance.

</p>

<h2>FAQ</h2>

<h3>Why is my wallet tracker not connecting?</h3>

<p>

The most common causes are low charge, the wrong tracker model for your phone, Bluetooth or location settings being off, unstable internet connection, or the tracker already being associated with another phone or account. Charge it, confirm the Apple or Samsung version, keep it close to your phone, and try setup again.

</p>

<h3>Can I use the Apple easyTag wallet tracker with a Samsung phone?</h3>

<p>

Choose the tracker that matches your phone ecosystem. The Wallet Tracker for Apple is compatible with Apple Find My devices, while the Wallet Tracker for Samsung is compatible with Samsung and Galaxy devices.

</p>

<h3>Do I need the easyTag charging cable?</h3>

<p>

Apple and Samsung easyTag card trackers use a USB-C to proprietary magnetic charging cable. If your original cable is lost or damaged, use the <a href="https://easytag.app/products/easytag-card-tracker-replacement-charging-cable">Additional / Replacement easyTag Charging Cable</a>.

</p>

<h3>Can I use an easyTag card to track a passport?</h3>

<p>

Yes, the <a href="https://easytag.app/products/passport-tracker-cover">Passport Tracker Cover for easyTag Card</a> includes built-in tracker card storage inside a passport holder. Set up and test the tracker before travel.

</p>

<h3>Can I attach an easyTag tracker card to luggage?</h3>

<p>

The <a href="https://easytag.app/products/luggage-tag-easytag-card-holder">Luggage Tag easyTag Card Holder</a> is designed to hold an easyTag tracker card securely and includes a steel cable attachment.

</p>

<h3>Does a tracker guarantee I will recover a lost item?</h3>

<p>

No. A tracker can help you keep track of important items through a compatible device setup, but it cannot guarantee recovery or prevent theft. Use it alongside normal precautions for wallets, passports, and luggage.
